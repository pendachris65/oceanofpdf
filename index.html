How to download books?

To download books, simply select your desired title and click the download button. Ensure you're logged into your account. The book will be saved to your device.

Is account creation necessary?

Yes, creating an account is necessary to access your downloaded books. It allows you to manage your library and track your purchases. Registration is quick and easy.

What payment method accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards and PayPal. This ensures a secure transaction for your purchases. Choose the method that suits you best at checkout.

Can i return books?

Due to the nature of digital downloads, we do not accept returns. However, if you experience issues with a download, please contact our support team. We are here to help resolve any problems.

How to reset password?
